How Group Classes Work

Our group classes are designed to provide an interactive and engaging learning experience. Here's how it works:

  • 1. Browse Classes: Explore our wide range of group classes available in different languages, levels, and time slots.
  • 2. Book Your Spot: Select a class that fits your schedule and book your spot. Classes have limited seats to ensure quality interaction.
  • 3. Join the Session: On the scheduled time, join the online classroom through our platform. All you need is a device with internet connection.
  • 4. Learn Together: Participate in interactive lessons, group discussions, and collaborative activities with your classmates and instructor.

Benefits of Group Learning

  • Learn from peer interactions
  • Practice speaking in a supportive environment
  • Get feedback from both instructor and peers
  • More affordable than private lessons
  • Build confidence through group participation
  • Meet like-minded learners

Perfect For Everyone

Our group classes are designed for learners of all backgrounds and goals:

  • Beginners: Start your learning journey in a supportive group environment where everyone is at a similar level.
  • Intermediate Learners: Enhance your skills through practice and interaction with peers at your level.
  • Advanced Students: Refine your abilities and maintain fluency through engaging group discussions.
  • Busy Professionals: Flexible scheduling allows you to fit learning into your busy lifestyle.

Learning Goals

  • Improve conversational skills
  • Prepare for language exams
  • Learn programming languages
  • Develop business communication
  • Build professional networks
  • Achieve personal growth
