Search a suitable tutor for you in our database. Find a suitable time slot according to your busy schedule and book your trial lesson. You can pay via credit card or PayPal. The last step is to enter the virtual classroom. If you like the tutor in your trial lesson then you can book 6, 12, 20, 40 lesson packages with the same teacher.

You can pay for lessons using credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) or PayPal. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system. Payment is required before the lesson begins to confirm your booking.

Standard lessons are typically 60 minutes long. However, some tutors may offer shorter 30-minute or longer 90-minute sessions. You can see the lesson duration when booking and select the option that works best for you.

All lessons take place in our virtual classroom, which is accessible through our platform. You can join from anywhere in the world using any device with internet connection - computer, tablet, or smartphone. No additional software installation is required.

For children under 13, we recommend parental supervision during the first few lessons. However, it's not mandatory for all lessons. Parents can monitor the session from a distance or be present as needed. For older children (13+), parental presence is optional but welcome.

Our tutors are trained to use visual aids, gestures, and simplified language to help children understand. Many tutors also speak multiple languages and can provide support in the child's native language when needed. You can filter tutors by language when searching to find one who speaks your child's language.
